About Anemia

Anemia occurs when your body lacks enough healthy red blood cells, leading to fatigue, weakness, and other symptoms.

Hemoglobin

Normal range: 13.2-16.6 g/dL (Men), 11.6-15 g/dL (Women)

MCH

Normal range: 27.5 - 33.2 pg

MCHC

Normal range: 33.4 - 35.5 g/dL

MCV

Normal range: 80 - 100 fL
